About Polania White Eagles

Our Team

Polonia White Eagles is a passionate and competitive soccer team based in Windsor, Canada. Founded with a commitment to excellence, teamwork, and sportsmanship, we take pride in representing our community on and off the field.

Our Home Ground

We play our home matches at Centerline Windsor, a premier soccer facility that provides the perfect environment for both training and competitive games. Our field is where we build our legacy, develop our skills, and create unforgettable moments.

Our Colors

Draped in red and white, our team embodies strength, unity, and determination. These colors reflect our spirit, heritage, and relentless pursuit of success.

Our Sponsors

We are proud to be supported by two incredible sponsors:

  • Fix Auto – A leader in automotive repair services, supporting Polonia White Eagles with reliability on and off the field.
  • Devollis – Fueling champions with best breakfast in town, proud sponsors of Polonia White Eagles.

Their unwavering support enables us to continue growing and striving for excellence in every match we play.

Our Mission

At Polonia White Eagles, our mission is to promote the love of soccer, foster young talent, and build a strong team that competes at the highest level. We believe in discipline, hard work, and the power of teamwork to achieve greatness.
